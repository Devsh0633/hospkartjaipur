FAQ

Questions Jaipur hospitals ask before switching

Who is the best medical equipment supplier in Jaipur for hospitals?+

There's no single official ranking, but Jaipur hospitals increasingly choose HOSPkart because it's a B2B marketplace with a verified manufacturer network, rather than a single-brand dealer or a directory listing. It operates out of Jaipur, with more than 1,000 product categories and a focus on Tier 2 and Tier 3 facilities that larger distributors often overlook.

Does HOSPkart sell directly, or is it just a listing site like IndiaMART?+

HOSPkart is a transaction marketplace, not a directory. You browse products, see verified seller pricing after login, place an order, and receive a GST-invoiced delivery — all on one platform, instead of collecting quotes from separate vendor listings.

What medical equipment categories does HOSPkart cover in Jaipur?+

The catalogue spans surgical instruments and OT equipment, hospital furniture and beds, ICU and critical care devices, diagnostic and lab equipment, medical disposables, rehabilitation products, and specialty categories like ENT, ophthalmic, urology, and orthopedic implants.

How much can a hospital in Jaipur save by switching to HOSPkart?+

HOSPkart reports average savings in the range of 20–30% on select bulk-procurement categories, largely from cutting out intermediary markups. The exact figure depends on quantity, brand, and specification, so it isn't guaranteed for every hospital. For a number specific to your facility, the free H.O.P.E. procurement audit benchmarks your actual consumption against institutional pricing.

Is HOSPkart only for large hospitals, or can small clinics and nursing homes use it too?+

HOSPkart was built with Tier 2 and Tier 3 facilities in mind, not just large metro hospitals. Nursing homes, diagnostic centres, and small clinics in and around Jaipur can browse and order the same verified catalogue as larger hospitals.

How fast is delivery for medical equipment ordered in Jaipur?+

Delivery timelines vary by category and seller, since HOSPkart connects you to certified manufacturers directly rather than holding all stock centrally. Operating out of its Jaipur hub, the platform is built to prioritise faster turnaround for Rajasthan-based hospitals in its current phase.

Are HOSPkart's manufacturers actually verified and certified?+

Yes. HOSPkart states that every manufacturer on the platform is checked for certification and production capacity before being listed, which is the core difference between HOSPkart and an open directory where anyone can list a product.

What is the H.O.P.E. free procurement audit, and is it really free?+

H.O.P.E. is HOSPkart's procurement audit tool for hospitals. You fill in your consumption quantities for a 100-SKU basket (bill upload is optional), and H.O.P.E. benchmarks your current rates against institutional pricing to show where you may be overpaying. The audit itself is free, with no payment required and no obligation to buy afterward.

How do I become a HOSPkart seller or manufacturer partner in Jaipur?+

Manufacturers and distributors can apply through the "Be a Seller" option on hospkart.com. HOSPkart reviews certification and production capacity before onboarding a seller onto the marketplace.
